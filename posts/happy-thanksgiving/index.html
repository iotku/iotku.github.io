Happy Thanksgiving!

Posted: 2013-11-27 23:43   |  More posts about speedrunning irl-stuff

Time flies fast!

In what seems like hard­ly any time it's al­ready the end of No­vem­ber. What do I have to show for it? As far as speedgam­ing goes I've made a few strides in run­ning Su­per Mario 64, main­ly get­ting a time un­der 20 min­utes for 16 star and hit my 1st year mark of speedrun­ning. I don't plan on do­ing 16 star any­more out­side of races be­cause I fig­ured out I don't re­al­ly en­joy it any­more. 16 Star is in­cred­i­bly repet­i­tive, small mis­takes count (even at the lev­el I'm at which is far from op­ti­mal) and BLJs, while a neat gim­mick, are an­noy­ing to mess up and some­times don't co­op­er­ate wast­ing a bunch of time for no good rea­son (It's not my fault­!.... that much).

As for re­al life (Oh how bor­ing...), things have been pret­ty un­event­ful for the last year, I've been out of col­lege stuff and done ba­si­cal­ly noth­ing. It's amaz­ing how easy it is to be un­pro­duc­tive! I hope to fix that in 2014 and bet­ter or­ga­nize my time so I can keep from get­ting too stupid, while leav­ing room for games and ac­tu­al­ly hav­ing a life. ­Some days I strug­gle to do any­thing out of what would seem to be pure lazi­ness; that needs to stop if I want to fin­ish col­lege, stay rea­son­ably smart, and avoid de­pres­sion as much as pos­si­ble.

I hope for the best in my com­ing year, best of luck to who­ev­er is read­ing this as well.

By the way, if you've got­ten this far (or skipped the block of tex­t... cheater!) I'm go­ing to put up ways to con­tact me in the nav­i­ga­tion bar pret­ty soon in case some­one wants to find me :p. Un­til then you can con­tact me on twit­ter @iotku
